About MATRX Health & Fitness

Where clinical expertise meets compassionate care.

Our Story

MATRX Health & Fitness was founded in 2017 by Dr. Krystina Miller, PT, DPT — a neurologic rehabilitation specialist who saw the same problem again and again: patients made real progress in therapy — but the system treated rehabilitation as a silo, not a bridge. Patients cycled through years of routine sessions without a clear path toward independence. Fitness options felt unsafe. Community felt distant. And there was no next chapter beyond the clinic.

So she built MATRX to bridge that gap — a place where clinical excellence, adaptive fitness, and community connection aren't separate programs, but one continuous path. Every patient works one-on-one with a Doctor of Physical Therapy, every visit.

Today, MATRX has grown into a specialized medical wellness practice serving Macomb County and beyond — grounded in evidence-based care, adaptive innovation, and the belief that physical therapy should prepare you for life beyond the clinic — not keep you dependent on it.
