Fitness & Strength

Training that meets you where you are — whether you're building strength after rehab or training for competition.

Neubie Strength Training

For patients and clients looking to build real strength, we offer one-on-one strength training sessions using the Neubie device. Grounded in the NeuFit methodology, these sessions combine targeted neuromuscular stimulation with real progressive overload — not cookie-cutter 3 sets of 10.

The result is measurable muscle adaptation and functional strength gains. Whether you're transitioning from rehab or pushing toward a new performance goal, every session is designed around your body and your objectives.

Adaptive Group Fitness

Every Friday from 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM, we host an adaptive group fitness class open to all abilities. Led by community partner Mike Jensen, these sessions focus on functional movement, community, and confidence.

No experience needed. No membership required. Just show up ready to move.

Drop-in sessions may be covered by some insurances for patients who have an active plan of care with us.

A Facility Built for Every Body

Our gym features wheelchair-accessible equipment, adaptive stations, and professional-grade strength training tools — all in a fully ADA-compliant space designed for rehabilitation and fitness alike.
