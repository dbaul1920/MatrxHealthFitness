The NeuFit Difference in Fitness

Every fitness program at MATRX is powered by NeuFit neurostimulation — the same technology used in our rehabilitation practice. By applying direct current stimulation during exercise, we help you recruit more muscle fibers per rep, reduce joint stress, and see measurable results faster than traditional training alone.

Whether you're joining a group cycle, booking private sessions, or dropping into a class — NeuFit is the engine behind your results.
