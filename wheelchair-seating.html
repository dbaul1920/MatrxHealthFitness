Wheelchair Seating & Positioning

Custom seating solutions for comfort, function, and independence.

The Right Fit Makes All the Difference

Proper wheelchair seating and positioning is critical for skin integrity, respiratory function, comfort, and overall quality of life. A poorly fitted wheelchair can lead to pressure injuries, pain, and reduced mobility.

Our certified seating specialists work with patients, caregivers, and equipment vendors to evaluate, recommend, and customize wheelchair seating systems that support function and prevent complications. Every evaluation and session is conducted one-on-one by a Doctor of Physical Therapy.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive seating and positioning evaluations
  • Pressure mapping assessments
  • Custom cushion and backrest selection
  • Power and manual wheelchair recommendations
  • Tilt, recline, and standing frame configurations
  • Coordination with DME vendors and insurance
  • Follow-up fittings and adjustments
  • Caregiver education and training

Who We Serve

Spinal Cord Injury

Seating systems designed for pressure relief, posture support, and functional independence.

Progressive Conditions

Adaptive solutions for MS, ALS, muscular dystrophy, and other evolving needs.

Transitional & Geriatric

Age-appropriate seating for aging adults and transitional patients ages 5 and older with complex positioning needs. For children under 5, availability is considered on a case-by-case basis.

Adaptive Sports Chair Fitting

Our team has specialized experience fitting adaptive sports wheelchairs and equipment — from racing chairs to everyday sport-specific setups — ensuring proper fit for both performance and safety.

Grant Writing & Funding Assistance

We assist patients with identifying and applying for grants to help fund adaptive equipment, reducing the financial barriers to mobility and independence.
